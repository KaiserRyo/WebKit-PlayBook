Two Audios

This file exists to test the interaction between two audio players.

  • Start one of the media players: verify that you can pause and restart it multiple times.
  • Let it play to the end: verify that you can restart it.
  • Start the second player while the first is already playing: verify that the second player starts and that the first is stopped automatically.
  • Restart the first, then restart the second, then restart the first, etc., in quick succession (i.e. with less than a second between them): verify that the next player restarts when told to, and that the currently-playing player always stops (so that there's never a time when both players are playing simultaneously).
